Scour (Mobile App UI) Scour is a concept app that generates a list of places to eat, determined by proximity, rating, and personal preferences.
My time in the student org Texas Convergent taught me to be problem-focused and to practice design thinking.

Scour birthed out of the problem my girlfriend and I were having of finding new places around us to eat at. I was inspired to create a prototype mobile UI design for this idea. Perhaps one day I can work with my developer friends and flesh this idea out into a viable product.

This project was good practice in planning out layouts, ideating value propositions, and creating components in Figma.
