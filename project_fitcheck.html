FitCheck (Desktop + Chrome ext.) Mix and match fits from any online store with FitCheck!

Save clothes into your Wardrobe with the FitCheck chrome extension, and use the FitCheck website to coordinate and share your outfits.

Out of 15 teams, our team of 5 people won “Best Overall Team” at Demo Day.
